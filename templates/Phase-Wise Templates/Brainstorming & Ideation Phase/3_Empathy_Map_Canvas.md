# Empathy Map Canvas

**Date:** 18 June 2026  
**Team ID:** Banka Akshay Kumar Reddy  
**Project Name:** ShopEZ (E-Commerce Platform)  

## Target User Persona 1: The Modern Online Shopper
Our primary consumers expect speed, security, and aesthetic appeal when browsing online stores.

*   **SAYS:** 
    *   "I just want a simple, fast way to buy what I need without jumping through unnecessary hoops."
    *   "I prefer shopping on my phone during my commute, so the site must look good on mobile."
*   **THINKS:** 
    *   "Is my payment and personal data secure here?"
    *   "Why does this site look so outdated? It makes me question the brand's reliability."
*   **DOES:** 
    *   Frequently browses stores on mobile devices.
    *   Compares prices across multiple tabs.
    *   Abandons the cart immediately if the checkout process requires too many steps.
*   **FEELS:** 
    *   Anxious about data privacy and security.
    *   Impatient when faced with slow page load times.
    *   Delighted by smooth micro-interactions, clean typography, and a native dark mode experience.

## Target User Persona 2: The Store Administrator
Business owners and managers who need to maintain the store without needing deep technical knowledge.

*   **SAYS:** 
    *   "I need to quickly update this product's price for a weekend sale and check recent orders."
    *   "I don't have time to learn a complicated new system just to manage my stock."
*   **THINKS:** 
    *   "I hope I don't accidentally break the live site by changing these settings."
    *   "I wish I could manage everything from the same website my customers see."
*   **DOES:** 
    *   Manages inventory on a daily basis.
    *   Checks order fulfillment statuses and updates promotional banners.
*   **FEELS:** 
    *   Stressed by overly complex management tools.
    *   Relieved and empowered when everything is consolidated into one unified, secure dashboard.
