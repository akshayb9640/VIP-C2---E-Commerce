## Problem Analysis
Understanding the core challenges faced by our dual user base—the shoppers and the store administrators—is crucial for delivering a platform that genuinely adds value.

| Problem Statement (PS) | I am (User Persona) | I’m trying to | But | Because | Which makes me feel |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PS-1** | A modern online shopper | discover products quickly, manage my cart effortlessly, and complete checkout smoothly | many competing websites are noticeably slow, difficult to navigate on mobile, or visually unappealing | they rely on bloated, outdated frameworks and fail to prioritize modern UX expectations like dark mode | deeply frustrated, often causing me to abandon my shopping cart and switch to a competitor. |
| **PS-2** | A store administrator | manage my product inventory, update pricing, and fulfill customer orders efficiently | my existing administrative tools are clunky, disconnected from the storefront, and have a steep learning curve | they require entirely separate legacy software or complex third-party integrations | overwhelmed, prone to making inventory errors, and less productive throughout the workday. |
